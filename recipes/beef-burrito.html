Beef Burrito

Description

This beef burrito is delicious. Servings, 4. Extra burritos can be frozen

Ingredients

  • Wheat tortilla
  • 1 lb Lean ground beef
  • Canned black beans
  • Avocado
  • Slicing tomato
  • Frozen corn
  • Sliced black olives
  • Pepperjack cheese

Instructions

  1. Finely dice the tomato into small cubes.
  2. Heat a sauce pan, and add olive oil or lard when hot.
  3. Put the ground beef in a sauce pan on medium heat until it begins to brown.
  4. When the beef is beginning to brown, add the frozen corn and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. When the beef is fully brown, add the sliced black olives, the beans, and the diced tomato. Cook for another 1 and 1/2 minutess and set aside.
  6. Slice the avacado into thin slices, and scoop out with a spoon. Grate enough pepperjack cheese for 4 burritos.
  7. Heat a large sauce pan on low heat with no oil.
  8. When hot, put a tortilla in the sauce pan. heat for about 30 seconds on each side, or until warm.
  9. Place several slices of avacado and some grated cheese inside the burrito. Then add the filling, and roll the burrito up. Repeat until all the filling has been used.
