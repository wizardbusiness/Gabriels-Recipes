Creamy Oatmeal

Description:

Delicious oats

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups quick dry oats
  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 1 cup filtered water
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • A banana
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1/4 cup unsalted trail mix w/dried cranberries
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Slice up the banana, and rinse the blueberries.
  2. Add the dry oats, oat milk, and water to a small pot and turn on the burner on medium heat.
    3. After about a minute, stir, and add in the butter. Turn down the heat to medium low.
  3. Continue stirring periodically until mixture is creamy and bubbling slightly.
    4. Turn off the heat, and add the sliced banana and the blueberries
  4. Serve in a large bowl, and add the trailmix on top.
